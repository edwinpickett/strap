Edwin Pickett

Composer

Music to me always felt like a constant movement. Getting from A to B, from the intro to the finale. What about the in-between? Well, anything can happen. This turns a simple movement into an adventure, a story, a myth. I came to appreciate this motion at an early age, thankfully exposed to a myriad of different aural colors.

At the same time, I was introduced to video games and wouldn't you know itâ€”taking a different form, there was that constant movement again. Video games tell a story of a character's journey, and that is essentially what music does as well. Of course, every video game is backed by its music. What makes this special is that this clash of different storytelling media turns into a veritable sea of immersion. You don't go around looking for a story. You get pulled into it. Playing my part in all of this, I give my all to help game developers kidnap unsuspecting players, drag them into their stories and make them feel like the true protagonists of their own virtual epics.
